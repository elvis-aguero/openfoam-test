# Numerical Parameters and Choices

## Geometry and Mesh
- **Type**: Unstructured tetrahedral mesh (generated by Gmsh).
- **Geometry Variants**:
  - `flat`: Vertical cylinder with flat bottom.
  - `cap`: Vertical cylinder with a hemispherical cap (pointing down).
- **Domain**: Vertical cylinder of diameter $D$ and height $H$.
- **Boundaries**:
  - `walls`: Side and bottom walls (no-slip, fixed flux pressure).
  - `atmosphere`: Top surface (open to atmosphere).

## Physics and Solver
- **Solver**: `interDyMFoam` (Multiphase VOF with dynamic mesh). The controlDict specifies `incompressibleVoF` which is likely an alias in this version, but `interDyMFoam` is the standard equivalent.
- **Multiphase**: VOF method (Volume of Fluid) tracking `alpha.water`.
- **Turbulence**: Laminar (assumed for sloshing unless Re is very high, but `transient` logic applies). Check `constant/momentumTransport` to verify.

## Initial Conditions
- **Water Level**: Height $H/2$ (half filled).
- **Velocity**: Initial rest ($U=0$).
- **Pressure**: Hydrostatic equilibrium approximated by uniform 0 ($p_{rgh}=0$).

## Motion
- **Type**: `sixDoFMotion` with prescribed motion via `table`.
- **Motion**: Circular translation in XY plane with Soft Start:
  $$ r(t) = \text{smootherstep}(t, t_{ramp}) \cdot r_{max} $$
  $$ x(t) = r(t) \cos(2\pi f t) $$
  $$ y(t) = r(t) \sin(2\pi f t) $$
  $$ z(t) = 0 $$
  No rotation.
- **Parameters**:
  - `r`: Radius of motion ($r_{max}$).
  - `f`: Frequency of motion.
  - `t_ramp`: Ramp duration (defaults to 10% of total duration) using Smootherstep ($6\tau^5 - 15\tau^4 + 10\tau^3$).

## Time Stepping
- **Scheme**: Euler or CrankNicolson (defined in `system/fvSchemes`).
- **Control**: Adaptive time stepping (`adjustTimeStep yes`).
- **Max Courant Number (`maxCo`)**: 0.5 (Conservative for VOF).
- **Max Alpha Courant (`maxAlphaCo`)**: 0.5 (Important for interface tracking).

## Gravity
- **Vector**: $(0, 0, -9.81)$ m/s$^2$. Aligned with cylinder axis (Z), pointing downwards.
